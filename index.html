How often should I have my air ducts cleaned?

The EPA recommends cleaning air ducts every 3 to 5 years, though more frequent cleaning is advisable if you have pets, allergies, asthma, recent home renovations or visible mold or dust buildup in your vents. Our licensed technicians can assess your system and recommend the best cleaning schedule for your Staten Island home or business.

Why is dryer vent cleaning so important?

Clogged dryer vents are one of the leading causes of house fires in the United States. Dangerous lint buildup restricts airflow, causes your dryer to overheat and creates a serious fire hazard. Regular dryer vent cleaning eliminates this risk, reduces drying times, lowers energy costs and extends the lifespan of your appliance — making it one of the most critical home safety services you can schedule.
